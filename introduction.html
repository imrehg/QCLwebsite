Introduction to Our Lab

Our laboratory has unique experience in building femtosecond frequency combs, enhanced by Taiwanese electronics and electro-optics industry. The group members have various backgrounds in electrical and mechanical engineering, physics and computer science, which helps us create advanced experiments in high precision spectroscopy and quantum control.
A few of our current research projects:
  • Femtosecond optical frequency comb, continuum generation
  • Compact cesium two-photon frequency standards
  • Investigation into wall-effect in vapor cells
  • Coherent population trapping with frequency comb in buffer cell and in magneto-optical trap
